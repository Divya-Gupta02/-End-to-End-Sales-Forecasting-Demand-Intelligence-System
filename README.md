

# 📊 End-to-End Sales Forecasting & Demand Intelligence System

### 📋 Project Overview
Every retail and e-commerce enterprise lives and dies by a critical operational question: **"How much of each product will we sell next month, and will we have enough stock to meet that demand?"** Miscalculations in either direction incur massive financial leaks—overstocking traps working capital and leads to high storage costs, while stockouts result in lost revenue and degraded customer retention.

This repository houses a production-grade **Demand Intelligence Engine** that leverages multi-layered time-series analysis, statistical models, machine learning algorithms, unsupervised anomaly detection, and cluster-based product segmentation to optimize supply chain inventory strategies.
